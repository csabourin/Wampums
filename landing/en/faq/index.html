Wampums FAQ

Answers for scout and youth group leaders evaluating a bilingual management platform.

Is Wampums bilingual by default?

Yes. Every marketing and product flow has English and French versions with locale-aware content.

Who is Wampums for?

Scout troops, guides, cadets, youth ministries, and community groups needing structured operations.

Can we use Wampums offline?

Yes. The PWA supports offline attendance capture with sync when internet is back.

How secure is data?

Wampums uses HTTPS, role-based access, and organization-level data isolation.
