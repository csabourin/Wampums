Built for weekly scout units

Every module supports real-world leadership workflows: fast preparation, clear follow-up, and strong parent collaboration.

Meeting planning

Plan weekly activities, materials, and responsibilities with a shared calendar.

Attendance & advancement

Capture attendance, points, and badge or honor progress.

Parent communications

Send updates, reminders, and documents so families stay aligned.

Role management

Assign roles (admins, leaders, parents) and secure access to data.

Documents & consents

Store health forms, permissions, and reports to help keep activities compliant.

Reports & export

Generate reports on participation, advancement, and fundraising (e.g., calendar sales).

Ready for every weekly meeting

A clear workflow to prepare, run, and archive meetings reliably.

Before the meeting

  • Add the activity, learning goal, and required resources.
  • Assign leaders and share the checklist with the team.
  • Send a reminder to parents with logistics.

During the meeting

  • Capture attendance and points live on mobile.
  • Log badge progress and any follow-up needs.
  • Record observations for the end-of-meeting report.

After the meeting

  • Share a recap with parents and the leadership team.
  • Update inventory, payments, and documents.
  • Plan the next meeting using the existing template.
