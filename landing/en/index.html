Frequently asked questions

How do we get started?

Request a demo and we’ll set up your organization. You can import existing participant data from CSV files and be running within a day.

How much does Wampums cost?

Wampums is free for scout groups during the early-access period. Contact us for details on future pricing.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use HTTPS (TLS), encrypted backups, and role-based access controls. Your data is isolated per organization and never shared with third parties.

Can we work in English and French?

Absolutely. Every screen, form, and notification is fully bilingual. Each user chooses their own language and the interface adapts instantly.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes. Wampums is a Progressive Web App that works on any phone, tablet, or computer — no app store download required. It even works offline for attendance and points.

Can we customize forms and fields?

Yes. The built-in form builder lets you create custom registration forms, health forms, and permission slips with conditional logic and bilingual labels.

Can we import our existing data?

Yes. Wampums supports CSV import for participants, guardians, and users. We can also help with data migration from other systems.

How does the team stay aligned each week?

Meeting plans, automatic reminders, shared dashboards, and communication tools keep leaders, parents, and volunteers in sync without extra effort.
