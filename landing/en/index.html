Ready for every weekly meeting

A clear workflow to prepare, run, and archive meetings reliably.

Before the meeting

  • Add the activity, learning goal, and required resources.
  • Assign leaders and share the checklist with the team.
  • Send a reminder to parents with logistics.

During the meeting

  • Capture attendance and points live on mobile.
  • Log badge progress and any follow-up needs.
  • Record observations for the end-of-meeting report.

After the meeting

  • Share a recap with parents and the leadership team.
  • Update inventory, budgets, and documents.
  • Plan the next meeting using the existing template.
